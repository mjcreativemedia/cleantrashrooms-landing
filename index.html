You Know The Problem Too Well

Trash rooms don’t fall apart overnight—they slip a little further every time the nightly crew can’t keep up with the spills, leaks, and bulk drop-offs.

By the time the calls and emails flood your inbox, the damage has already started. Here’s how fast the situation snowballs without a specialty team.

The trash room decline timeline

  • Week 1: Surface grime shows up. Spills and leaks seep into porous surfaces, inviting bacteria and pests.
  • Week 3: Odors spark complaints. Uncontrolled odors migrate into hallways and elevators, lowering tenant satisfaction.
  • Week 6: Safety risks rise. Mold, slippery floors, and pest activity create health violations and liability exposure.
  • Week 8+: Costs surge. Corroded chutes, stained walls, and infested rooms require expensive overhauls.
