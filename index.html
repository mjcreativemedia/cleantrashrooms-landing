How Trash Room Problems Escalate

Interrupting the cycle early saves thousands in remediation costs and protects your reputation.

  1. Week 1: Surface grime appears

    Spills and leaks seep into porous surfaces, inviting bacteria and pests.

  2. Week 3: Odors spark complaints

    Uncontrolled odors migrate into hallways and elevators, lowering tenant satisfaction.

  3. Week 6: Safety risks rise

    Mold, slippery floors, and pest activity create health violations and liability exposure.

  4. Week 8+: Costly damage accrues

    Corroded chutes, stained walls, and infested rooms require expensive overhauls.
